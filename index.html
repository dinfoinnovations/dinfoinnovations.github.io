Who Are We

  • We are a telecom start-up founded by industry experts with extensive experience in network planning, roll-out, and operations.
  • Our team consists of former CTOs from leading telecom firms in India, deeply involved in the ongoing digital transformation. Each of our three founder directors brings over 35 years of expertise in electronics, telecom, and data communication services.
  • Specializing in telecommunication networks and cutting-edge technologies such as 3G, 4G, 5G, and wireless, WIFI implementations, we are seasoned professionals in the field.
  • Our expertise extends to fibre planning, FTTH, and NLD rollouts, ensuring comprehensive coverage of telecom infrastructure.
  • Recognized as a technology start-up under Odisha Start-up Policy 2016, we are committed to developing innovative products and services. Our collaboration with IIT Bhubaneswar for product development and incubation underscores our dedication to driving forward-thinking solutions in the telecom industry.
